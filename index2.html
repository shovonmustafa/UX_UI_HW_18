Hi, I'm Malini!

I am a UX/UI designer, Researcher, and Problem Solver. I specialize in product management. I enjoy problem solving and working as a team to create a product that makes life easier for users.

Projects

Government Redesign

This government redesign was to redesign the home page layout and UI to make it easier for the user to navigate the website. The original website was cluttered and a lot of the navigation did not align with headings.

AllergyBud App

This project was to create an app for users with food allergies that helps them find quality places to eat in their area. We merged Yelp and Find Me Gluten Free to make an easily accessible app that helps users with allergies.

Coding Portfolio

In this project I used my basic knowledge of coding to create a portfolio for myself. I used HTML, CSS, and Javascript to write this. I am still working on my coding, but I have a general understanding of the different languages.

Skills

Prototyping and Creating Wireframes

I enjoy creating wireframes and prototypes. When playing around with different ideas you can really see what works and what doesn't. Taking an idea and creating a prototype is one of my favorite things about UX/UI design.

Communication

My communication skills are one of my best qualities. I am a stickler for making sure everyone involved in a project is in the loop and up to date. If communication is poor then the end result will also be poor.

Agile Methodologies

In my previous role I was trained to use the Agile approach on projects and I started to really like it. It made working through projects much more organized and easy to accomplish. And as always... iterating is key!

Information Architecture

I enjoy information architecture is because it is all about oragization. You go through a website and take it apart, then reorganize it like a puzzle. When redesigning a webiste I focused on making the redesign clean and easy to navigate.
